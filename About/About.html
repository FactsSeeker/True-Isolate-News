Who are we?

Isolate News is an organization built around providing you the most amount of information with the least amount of bias. We take pride in not only giving a large spectrum of perspectives for every topic, but also in providing information that's free from personal bias. Additionally, Isolate News prioritizes user safety and privacy. No information of user data is stored.


How do we achieve this?

We only use strict facts gathered from either outside sources which are then stripped of unneed perspectives or from the actual scene of events. Additionally we also host discussion areas for free where journalists can be heard and are free from censorship.
